Understanding the Result

The classifier has analyzed the text patterns, structure, and linguistic features to make its determination.

  • Human-written: Text that shows typical human writing patterns including natural inconsistencies, personal style, and contextual awareness.
  • AI-generated: Text that shows patterns commonly found in AI outputs, such as particular word choices, sentence structures, or stylistic consistency.

Confidence score indicates how certain the model is about its classification. The higher the percentage, the more confident the model is in its assessment.

Note: This tool provides an educated assessment but is not perfect. Highly skilled writers can sometimes be misclassified as AI, and well-crafted AI text might occasionally be classified as human.

About This Classifier

This tool uses a fine-tuned RoBERTa model to analyze text and determine whether it was likely written by a human or generated by an AI system like GPT or Claude.

The classifier examines patterns in word choice, sentence structure, and other linguistic features to make its assessment.

How it works:

  • Input text: Enter or paste any text you want to analyze.
  • AI detection: Our model analyzes subtle patterns in language usage.
  • Results: Get an assessment of whether the text is likely human-written or AI-generated.

Note: While this classifier is trained on a diverse dataset, it may not be 100% accurate for all types of text. The longer and more complex the text, the more accurate the classification tends to be.
